Dead of Night - Page 1

I was making dinner for my kids when it came. Our dog, Velvet, started barking at the front door and I asked my eldest, Maria, to see why. Maria had left the room and then there was a loud crash and Velvet stopped barking abruptly, accompanied by a shriek from Maria.

“Mom! Mom!” She came sprinting back into the kitchen, blood splattered on her purple nightgown, and a horrified look on her face.
