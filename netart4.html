A Lily or a Nightshade - Details Page

This project uses a poem that I wrote a couple years ago. A Lily or a Nightshade describes how two things that seem similar on the outside can actually be vastly different, such as two flowers while one is deadly. The images I used reflect each part of the poem and sets a scene for it as well.

I used stickpng.com for sourcing all of my images to usein this project. I used vecteezy.com for the nightshade image on the third page.
