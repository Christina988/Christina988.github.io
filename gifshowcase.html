This page is a collection of the GIFs I have completed. I hope you enjoy!

This animation unlike the others, was very difficult to do. Out of these first three, this one took me the longest to finish. I knew I wanted to play with perspective and a moving camera angle, I also wanted the camera to move somewhat quickly to follow the motorcycle. I like the vibe of vehicle lights at night time, and I had never done a drawing or animation with a black background before this one, so I decided to go for it. I used Procreate for this, and I wanted this to be a mixture of calm with the lights driving by but it also has a lot of energy in it because of the speed of the motorcycle and the speed of the camera moving.

This is another animation I did for my project. This one was slightly difficult because of the transitions between emotions, neutral, surprise, anger, then a deep breath to calm down. I also had to figure out how to make each emotion stay there long enough to be recognizible. I love eyes and how much emotion they can show on their own. This particular animation shows just how quickly emotions can change from one moment to the next. I also used Procreate for this.

This is a quick animation I did of a small spec flying around that ended up exploding like a firework. It is a whimisical piece and has high energy and excitment. I used the procreate application to do the animation, and I have messed around with animating fireworks a couple times before so that previous knowledge helped me create this.
